INTRODUCTION

Ziggurat '25 is an annual gathering of professionals, academics, and students in the field of civil engineering, aimed at fostering collaboration, knowledge exchange, and innovation in sustainable construction practices.

We invite your esteemed organization to partner with us for the upcoming symposium scheduled on March 22 at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, promising a day of learning, networking, and inspiration.

SYMPOSIUM OVERVIEW

Featuring keynote presentations, technical sessions, poster presentations, and hands-on workshops, Ziggurat '25 promises to be a platform for learning and networking with industry leaders.

Attendees will have the opportunity to interact with renowned experts and learn about the latest advancements in civil engineering, with special focus on sustainable infrastructure development and smart cities.
